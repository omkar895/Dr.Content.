# Requirement Document: Dr.Content

## 1. Project Overview
**Dr.Content** is an AI-powered Multi-Platform Content Optimizer designed to help creators, marketers, and brands scale their presence across social media and the web. By leveraging advanced LLMs, it transforms a simple topic into a comprehensive content strategy.

## 2. Problem Statement
Content creation is time-consuming and platform-specific. A post that works on LinkedIn often fails on Twitter or Instagram. Creators struggle to:
- Adapt their tone for different audiences.
- Generate engaging hooks that stop the scroll.
- Identify the best posting strategies and SEO keywords.
- Repurpose a single idea across multiple channels efficiently.

## 3. Core Features
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Optimized templates for LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter (X), YouTube, and Blog posts.
- **Dynamic Tone Selection**: Choose from Professional, Casual, Storytelling, Witty, and more to match brand voice.
- **AI Content Engine**: Powered by Google Gemini to generate high-quality, structured content.
- **Strategic Blueprint**: Beyond just text, it provides:
    - Scrolling-stopping **Hooks**.
    - Clear **Calls to Action (CTA)**.
    - Optimized **Hashtags** and **SEO Keywords**.
    - **Repurposing Suggestions** for cross-platform growth.
- **Premium UI/UX**: A modern, responsive, and aesthetically pleasing interface for a seamless experience.

## 4. Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: React 19 (TypeScript)
- **Build Tool**: Vite 6
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **AI Integration**: Google Generative AI (@google/genai)
- **Deployment**: Production-ready for platforms like Vercel or Netlify.

## 5. Target Audience
- Individual Content Creators & Influencers
- Small to Medium Business Owners
- Social Media Managers
- Digital Marketing Agencies
